About me

Computers and technology have been an integral part of my life ever since I was young, and from a young age, I played a lot of video games, both by myself and with friends. As I grew up and began to develop more career-related aspirations, my love for video games lead me to want to learn how to make my own games, which naturally lead to programming. Since then, I have self taught a number of programming languages, and learned even more throughout my educational career, and my desire to make video games developed into a passion for programming and problem solving. Nowadays, I still enjoy playing games with friends, and have developed a passion for cooking, and especially eating, good food.
